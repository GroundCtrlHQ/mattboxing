# About Page

## Purpose
The About Page provides comprehensive information about Matt Goddard ("The Boxing Locker"), showcasing his credentials, experience, and teaching philosophy to build trust and credibility with users.

## Key Features

### Hero Section
- **Name & Brand**: Prominent display of "Matt Goddard" and "The Boxing Locker"
- **Tagline**: "7-0 Pro, National Champion" - immediate credibility
- **Visual Identity**: Consistent branding with boxing red accent

### Professional Biography
- **20+ Years Experience**: Highlights extensive ring experience
- **Undefeated Record**: 7-0 professional boxing record
- **National Champion**: Championship credentials displayed prominently
- **Career Focus**: Emphasis on mastering art and science of boxing

### Teaching Philosophy
- **"Value of Looks"**: Core philosophy explained:
  - Proper form isn't just aesthetic
  - Focus on efficiency, power, and injury prevention
  - Biomechanical precision
- **Technical Approach**: Combines technical precision with practical wisdom

### The Five Boxing Philosophies
Detailed breakdown of Matt's core teaching framework:

1. **Brain**: Strategic thinking and fight IQ
2. **Legs**: Footwork, movement, and positioning
3. **Hands**: Technique, speed, and power
4. **Heart**: Courage, determination, and will
5. **Ego**: Confidence balanced with humility

Each philosophy displayed with:
- Visual icon/circle
- Name
- Description

### Achievements Display
- **7-0 Professional Record**: Undefeated career highlight
- **National Champion**: Championship achievement
- **Visual Cards**: Trophy and award icons for visual appeal

### Call-to-Action
- **Explore Video Library**: Direct link to videos page
- **Engagement**: Encourages users to explore content
- **Conversion Path**: Natural progression to using platform

## Design Elements
- **Card-Based Layout**: Information organized in digestible cards
